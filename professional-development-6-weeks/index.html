About BJC and This Workshop

BJC is designed to meet the requirements of the College Board AP Computer Science Principles curriculum framework. It was originally developed as a university breadth course at the University of California, Berkeley, and has been extensively revised for high school use at EDC (Education Development Center).

This is a six-week workshop. The first and last weeks are face-to-face; during the middle four weeks, most of the work is done online, along with a computer-mediated virtual meeting once per week. The materials needed for the workshop are all online, with links to them on this page.
